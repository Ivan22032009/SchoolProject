Our big goal

We strive to create an environmentally conscious society where every student and resident of Uman actively participates in collecting and sorting garbage. Our goal is not only to reduce waste, but also to teach proper resource management, recycling and reuse. Together we can make the city cleaner and ensure a safer future for future generations.

About the EcoFast project

Idea and Beginning

EcoFast is an initiative that unites the community, student groups and businesses of Uman with the aim of forming a sustainable environmental culture. The project was born from the desire to teach children from an early age to care for the environment and involve adults in joint activities.

Areas of Activity

  • Conducting master classes and eco-classes for children and adults.
  • Organization of promotions, competitions and events on recycling.
  • Expansion of the network of collection points for the convenience of recycling.
  • Information campaigns on conscious consumption and reuse of things.

Future Plans

In collaboration with local businesses and the city council, we aim to expand our impact, organize more eco-activities, and make the recycling process as convenient as possible for residents. We believe that even the smallest steps contribute to global changes for the benefit of nature.
