About EcoFast

EcoFast is not only a useful online platform, but also a powerful tool for environmental change. Thanks to the active participation and support of the public, the city is moving towards an environmentally responsible future! 💚 Join the change - let's make the city cleaner together!

🌿 Environmental awareness

We teach residents how to properly sort their garbage and understand the importance of recycling.

♻️ Processing

We simplify the process of handing over recyclables through a convenient network of collection points.

🤝 Community

We unite people who care about the environment and the future of their city.

Eco-challenge "Clean City in 90 Days"

How does it work?

1. Register by clicking "Login"🤘

Create an account on our platform to start collecting points.

2. Sort and recycle your recyclables🚮.

Drop off your recyclables at one of the collection points.

3. Take photos📸 and upload photos📲

Upload a photo in your account showing the fact of recycling.

4. Get points1️⃣

For each photo uploaded, get 1 point.

5. Get a prize🎉🎁

Once every 90 days, the winners of the eco-challenge will be determined and will receive eco-gifts.

Why is this important?

🌍 For the environment

Recycling helps reduce the amount of waste in landfills and reduce environmental pollution.

🏙️ For the city

A clean city means a more comfortable life for all its residents.

💚 For you

Do your part in preserving nature.

Join us!

Together we can make Uman a cleaner and more environmentally conscious city. Start sorting your waste today!
